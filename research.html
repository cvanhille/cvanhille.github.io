Research

My research focuses on modelling cell motility, intracellular self-organization, and out-of-equilibrium dynamics. I also explore applications of theoretical modelling in societal contexts, such as refugee dynamics and infectious disease transmission and prevention.

Projects

  • Agent-based simulations of migration dynamics
